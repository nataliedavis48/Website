Sam: Yes. Most countries, including the United States, eventually decided to recognize the government in Beijing as the official government of China. However, they continued to keep close, unofficial ties with Taiwan.

Sam: And it’s a massive player in the global economy, especially when it comes to technology. Most of the advanced computer chips used in our phones and cars are made in Taiwan.
