There were different waiting rooms at bus stations, and even different water fountains. Usually, the facilities for Black people were not as good as the ones for white people.

Alex: For more than a year, Black people in that city refused to use the buses. They walked to work, sometimes for many miles, or they shared cars. This was a way to protest without using any violence.
