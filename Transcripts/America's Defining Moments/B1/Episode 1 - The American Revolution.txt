Alex: Exactly. The colonists were angry because they had no representatives in the British Parliament in London. Alex: Yes. France wanted to weaken the British Empire, so they sent ships, soldiers, and money to help the Americans. This turned the local conflict into a global war. Finally, in 1781, the British army surrendered at Yorktown. Two years later, a treaty was signed, and the United States was officially recognized as an independent nation.
