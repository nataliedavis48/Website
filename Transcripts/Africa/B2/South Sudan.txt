Sam: The West, especially countries like the United States, Norway, and the UK, played a big role in helping South Sudan become independent. Today, they provide a lot of humanitarian aid. On the other hand, South Sudan joined the East African Community, so it is trying to build stronger ties with neighbors like Kenya and Uganda.

But despite this, it is one of the poorest countries in the world in terms of the daily lives of its people. Most of the population depends on farming for survival, and the wealth from oil hasn't really reached the average person due to war and corruption.
